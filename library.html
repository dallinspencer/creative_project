Welcome to Emotion Capture

Emotion capture is a library of emotions describing where these emotions come from, and how they are expressed. Images of people expressing these emotions are also given.

The crown jewel of emotion capture is the Comparison tool. The library of emotion images has been used to train a program which can identify emotions expressed by a person in an image.
